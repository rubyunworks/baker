RecipeCollection

this is a psudeo recipe. it does not represent a single program on its own but rather only serves as means to collect other receipes together (under dependencies) a recipe collection just pretends to do something. but really only its dependencies actually do. (this may change in the future to allow post install proceessing at a collection level. not sure.)
